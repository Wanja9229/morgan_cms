psychology Personality

Eldric is often seen as aloof and distant, a trait born from years of solitary study in the high towers of the Arcane University. However, beneath his cold exterior lies a fierce loyalty to those he considers friends. He has a penchant for sarcastic remarks and a dangerously short temper when dealing with ignorance. His obsession with ancient history often leads him into dangerous ruins where others fear to tread.
